Jonathan Ma is developing research directions around Bayesian Vector Autoregressions, robust forecasting, and uncertainty-aware modeling.

A major area of interest is extending Bayesian VAR frameworks to better handle model misspecification, distributional shifts, and non-Gaussian innovation behavior.

This includes interest in methods such as distributionally robust optimization, residual modeling, and generative approaches for representing uncertainty in time series systems.

His broader research interests connect Bayesian statistics, forecasting, optimization, and machine learning.
