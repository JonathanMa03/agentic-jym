Title: Interests
Source Type: interests

Overview:
Jonathan Ma’s interests lie at the intersection of statistics, machine learning, and applied decision-making, with a focus on building robust, data-driven systems.

Primary Interests:
Bayesian data analysis, applied machine learning algorithms, generative modeling for image and audio data, empirical evaluation of economic theory, stock price forecasting, and automated data pipeline and script design.

Technical Interests:
Transfer learning, diffusion models, vector autoregressions, hidden Markov models, sentiment analysis, inverse probability weighting, quality-adjusted life years (QALY) modeling, individual patient data reconstruction, high-dimensional genomics, low-rank adaptation (LoRA), and recurrent neural networks such as LSTMs.

Application Areas:
Healthcare analytics, business analytics, human–computer interaction, financial modeling, biometrics, and quantitative finance.

Long-Term Goals:
Develop robust, data-driven optimization frameworks and contribute to the advancement of explainable, reliable, and fair AI systems for high-stakes decision-making.
