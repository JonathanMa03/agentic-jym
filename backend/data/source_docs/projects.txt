Project: Predictors of Academic Performance: The Role of Alcohol Consumption and Gender
Description:
Analyzed the relationship between alcohol consumption and academic performance using student-level data.
Problem:
Determine whether alcohol use impacts grades and whether gender moderates this relationship.
Methods:
Linear regression with interaction terms, ANOVA, residual diagnostics, Shapiro–Wilk and Breusch–Pagan tests.
Technologies:
R, dplyr, ggplot2, RMarkdown.
Results:
Weekend alcohol consumption negatively impacted performance, especially for male students, with statistically significant interaction effects.
