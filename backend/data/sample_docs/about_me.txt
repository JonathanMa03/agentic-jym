Jonathan Ma is a Master of Science in Engineering student in Applied Mathematics and Statistics at Johns Hopkins University.

He has an academic background in finance, economics, and statistics, and is interested in combining statistical modeling, machine learning, and optimization for real-world decision-making problems.

His interests include Bayesian statistics, time series forecasting, probabilistic machine learning, distributionally robust optimization, and generative modeling.

He is especially interested in research and applications at the intersection of forecasting, uncertainty quantification, and data-driven decision-making.
